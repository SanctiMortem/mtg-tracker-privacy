Privacy Policy

MTG Commander Tracker · Last updated: April 17, 2026

Who we are

MTG Commander Tracker ("the app") is developed by About Agency. Contact: gabassem@gmail.com.

What we collect

  • Account data: email address and the OAuth identifier returned by your sign-in provider (Apple, Google, or email/password), handled by our authentication provider Clerk.
  • Gameplay data you create in the app: player names, deck names, commanders, matches, match events, and pod (group) memberships.
  • Preferences: language, theme, default life total, and similar settings.

What we do NOT collect

  • We do not run ads.
  • We do not sell or share your data with third parties for advertising or marketing.
  • We do not collect location, contacts, photos, or device identifiers for tracking.
  • We do not use third-party analytics or tracking SDKs.

Where your data lives

Authentication is provided by Clerk. Gameplay data is stored in a PostgreSQL database hosted on Neon. Both providers act as data processors on our behalf.

How your data is used

Solely to provide the app's features: signing you in, saving your matches and decks, computing your stats, and sharing pod data with pod members you explicitly invite.

Your rights

You can sign out at any time from Settings. You can permanently delete all your match history from Settings → Clear match history. To request full account deletion or a copy of your data, email gabassem@gmail.com; we will process the request within 30 days.

Children

The app is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes

We may update this policy. Material changes will be reflected by the "Last updated" date above.
